M.S. Hasinie has achieved her 4th entry into the INDIA BOOK OF RECORDS by successfully reciting 1-100 square numbers while hula hooping on knees in 2 min 34 sec for the title “FASTEST CHILD TO RECITE SQUARES FROM 1 TO 100 WHILE PERFORMING KNEE HULA HOOP SPINS”. This is her 7th record overall.

She has already successfully set records in multiple categories, including:

  • Science: Reciting periodic table elements while balancing on inline skates and hula hooping.
  • General Knowledge: Reciting all countries and capitals while Hula hooping and solving 7 different Rubik’s Cubes Simultaneously.
  • Intellectual: Solving 7 Rubik’s cube in 7 min.
  • Social : Identifying 150 countries by looking at their map outlines.

– Guinness World Record: Identifying the maximum number of vehicle logos in 1 minute.

And now, she has achieved 7th record in Mathematics by reciting 1-100 square numbers while hula hooping on knees in 2 min 34 sec.
